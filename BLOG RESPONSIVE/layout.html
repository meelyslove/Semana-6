A Great Year For Gaming History

This is almost the end of 2017, and we´re totally hyped about the upcoming titles for 2018, but let´s not forget about the experience we had this year with the amazing games we played for hours and hours and had fun with and if you haven´t play any of these amazing titles, worry not. Because Gamer Selection has prepared for you a list in no speciffical order for GREAT GAMES THAT MADE 2017 THE BEST YEAR FOR GAMING HISTORY! We had Such great games and many 10/10 Masterpieces within our reviews and many other forums have noticed the same we do now. Here are the best of 2017:

Best of 2017

-The Legend of Zelda: Breath of The Wild

Winner of Game of the Year 2017, Nintendo made a hit going back in time to bring back one of everyone´s favorite saga, evolving the open world and a new way of playing but not forgetting what playing Zelda feels like, MASTERPIECE 10/10.



-HELLBLADE: Senua´s Sacrifice

Indie game which director played as protagonist (Senua) to make sure everything goes great. And sure it did! 9/10.



-Horizon Zero Dawn

Explore a vibrant world rich with the beauty of nature but inhabited by aweinspiring, highly advanced machines. Nominated for Game of Year and a PlayStation 4 Exclusive game, Guerrilla Game reinvented them selves with this MASTERPIECE 10/10.



-Cuphead

The all-cartoon Magical Wondergame! Cuphead is a classic run and gun action game heavily focused on boss battles. Inspired by cartoons of the 1930s, the visuals and audio are painstakingly created with the same techn iques of the era, i.e. traditional hand drawn cel animation, watercolorbackgrounds, and original jazz soundtrack for the game. 10/10



-Nier: Automata

Invaders from another world attack without warning, unleashing a new type of threat: weapons known as “machine lifeforms.” In the face of this insurmountable threat, mankind is driven from Earth and takes refuge on the Moon. The Council of Humanity organizes a resistance of android soldiers in an effort to take back their planet. To break the deadlock, the Resistance deploys a new unit of android infantry: YoRHa 10/10.



Persona 5

Out of Shin Megami Tensei Saga, Persona comes back on its 5th game. This game came back with an unique RPG style that makes you wish all rpg´s where the same. Incredible Graphics, Unique Soundtrack and great Story. Nominated to Game of The Year, Persona 5 is a 10/10. MASTERPIECE.



UNCHARTED 4: The Lost Legacy

This DLC from the previous game launched in 2016 UNCHARTED 4 A thief´s end comes with the background story of Chloe from Uncharted 2 and Nadine from Uncharted 4. Join them into the great adventure that holds a great story and gameplay. 9/10
